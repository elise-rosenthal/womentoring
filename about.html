Our Inspiration:
Mary Fernandez

About Mary

Born and raised in the great city of New York , Mary Fernandez is known as one of the Greatest minds in the STEM field. In her years as an Undergraduate at Brown University, Fernandez studied Computer Science, ultimately receiving both a B.A. and an M.S. degree. Post-Undergrad life led Fernandez to apply and enroll in the computer science program at Princeton University’s Graduate program, from which she earned a Ph.D. in Computer Science. In her working years, Fernandez has contributed to ACM SIGMOD as a secretary and treasurer, as well as working as the associate editor for ACM Transactions on Database Systems. Additionally, she has served on the board on the Computing Research Association. One of her greatest achievements was her ownership of “Mentornet” where she became CEO. Atop all this, Fernandez was awarded the “Great Minds in STEM Technical Achievement in Industry Award”, with much thanks to the quality of her work. As the CEO of Mentornet, a site that helps people find mentors in their preferred fields, Fernandez’s work ties into our work thematically. While her work is more widespread, our work would be aimed towards younger generations of women who want to enter STEM fields.

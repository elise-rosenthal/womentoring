Core Beliefs

Empowerment

All women have the power to do whatever they put their minds to. Empowering women globally to dream big in STEM helps bring in the technology of the future.

Financially Feasible

No woman should be held back from her dreams because of her finances. Womentoring is free to all, because we believe that all minds are powerful, no matter their economic status.

Online and IRL

While our program is primarily digital, throughout the year mentors and mentees will have opportunities to meet in person through organized gatherings.
